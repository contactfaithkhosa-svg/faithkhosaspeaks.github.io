About Faith

The oil was already
in the house.

From childhood, Faith Khosa demonstrated a gift that could not be contained — she would take chalk from school just so she could teach her friends what she had learned that day. That impulse to learn deeply and pour it into others has never left her. It just needed a bigger room.

Today, Faith is a Speaker, Educator, and Preparation Coach based in South Africa. She believes that the greatest tragedy is not failing — it is arriving at the moment unprepared. Her mission is simple and profound: equip people to be ready before the opportunity arrives.

Faith speaks to students, young adults, aspiring entrepreneurs, and purpose-driven women — across schools, churches, conferences, and corporate spaces. She does not deliver generic motivation. She delivers targeted preparation: researching every audience she serves and arriving with a message that speaks directly to where they are.

"What do you have in your house?"

— 2 Kings 4:2 · The question that changes everything
